3M's Catering and Food Services

3M's Catering and Food Services is in charge of event preparation and provision of culinary services for event planners at remote locations such as hotels, restaurants, etc. The company provides several services that may make the event worth to enjoy.

Since Filipinos are commonly known to set up parties and gatherings during events, clients may arrange an agreement and expect 3M's Catering and Food Services to offer assistance to make the events fun and entertaining.

Ensuring quality service
Looking Forward in making business with you!

1. Do you offer delivery services and is there a delivery charge?

  • Yes, we offer delivery services! However, the delivery charge depends on the location and distance. If you're interested in a specific venue, feel free to chat with us for a quote.

2. Are there additional charges for setup and takedown services?

  • Yes, for upstairs deliveries or setups that require extra effort, there may be an additional charge. We'll clarify this during your consultation to ensure transparency.

3. What is your service area?

  • Our primary service area covers from Villamor Pasay City to Alabang, but we can cater to special requests depending on the season and availability. Just send us a message to discuss your specific needs.

4. Do you offer event packages or do I need to customize everything?

  • We have some pre-designed packages covering essential components like tables, chairs, and service staff. However, we also specialize in custom orders. Let us know your vision and we'll create a personalized package just for you!

5. What services do you include in your packages?

  • Our packages typically include chairs, tables, food catering, service staff like waiters and waitresses, and optional additions like clowns or sound systems. You can discuss the specific inclusions and customize them based on your needs.

6. Do you have any special limitations during peak seasons?

  • During major holidays like Christmas and New Year, our service range may be slightly restricted due to high demand. Additionally, delivery may only be available from Makati, Taguig, and Pasay for peak season bookings.
